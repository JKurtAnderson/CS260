We've Been Listening

Students have been telling us for years that they hate coming down to the basement of the Talmage building. We don't blame you! It seems almost antiquated for any class to not be handled remotely now-a-days, let alone a CS class. Well, fret no more. This website hosts remote pass off drivers for most of the labs!

Freedom to Work Anywhere

You're a busy person. CS 240 is not the only thing that you've got on your plate. Maybe you like to work from home, or in the library, or during hours that the TA's don't work. These remote servers enable you to work from anywhere. No longer will you be tied down to one location. We hope that these tools help you to make more effective usage of your time.

We've Got You Covered

Don't worry, we're still sticking around to help you! This is only an optional tool for students who want to pass off remotely. The TA's will remain in the TA office to help you with any issues you encounter.
